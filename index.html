Dr. Vinay Shankar Pandey

Dr. Vinay Shankar Pandey is a dedicated and accomplished individual currently serving as an Assistant Professor at the esteemed National Institute of Technology in Delhi, India. With a strong background in the Department of Applied Sciences, Vinay Shankar Pandey has made significant contributions to the field of electromagnetic wave research, particularly in plasma and dielectric media.Another prominent aspect of Vinay's research involves dielectric resonator antennas, an area where he has made notable advancements.

Vinay's research interests are diverse and span across various fascinating areas of science. He has a keen focus on electromagnetic waves in plasma and dielectric media, a topic of paramount importance in our increasingly interconnected world. His work in this domain contributes to the understanding of how these waves propagate and interact in different mediums, which has implications in various fields, including telecommunications and materials science.Vinay Shankar Pandey has delved into cutting-edge research.
